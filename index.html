Heritage

Three generations
of maritime mastery.

1938 The Bilgin shipyard opens on the banks of the Golden Horn, Istanbul.
1972 First international commission — a 42 m vessel for a European collector.
2001 Launch of the Bilgin 156 at 47 m, establishing a new standard for Turkish shipbuilding.
2019 Delivery of the Bilgin 263 — at 80 m, the largest superyacht ever built in Turkey.

Since 1938, the Bilgin family has worked from the shores of the Bosphorus — where East meets West and the ancient meets the modern. Every vessel we build carries that spirit: a commitment to craft that does not compromise, a vision that does not follow trends, and a belief that the best work is always built to last.
